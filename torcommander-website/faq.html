Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is TorCommander?

TorCommander is a Linux tool that routes your terminal commands through the Tor network to hide your IP address and location.

❓ Do I need to install Tor separately?

Yes. You must have Tor installed and running on your system for TorCommander to work correctly.

❓ Does TorCommander support Windows or macOS?

No. Currently, TorCommander is designed for Linux-based systems only.

❓ Is this tool legal to use?

Yes. Using Tor to protect your privacy is legal in most countries, but always use the tool ethically and in accordance with your local laws.

❓ Where can I report bugs?

You can report bugs on the GitHub Issues page.

❓ Can I contribute?

Absolutely! Head over to the Contributing page to learn how to get started.
