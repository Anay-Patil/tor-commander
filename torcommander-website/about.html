About TorCommander

TorCommander is a unique privacy-enhancing tool created to route Linux commands through the Tor network for improved anonymity and geolocation masking. Built as a tribute and extension of the work by YouTuber Dr. Jones Birch, this project continues his vision of accessible, open-source privacy tools.

Our goal is to provide developers, researchers, and enthusiasts with a modular, extensible, and secure framework for executing system commands without revealing their digital footprint.

This is not just a tool it's an invitation to experiment, improve, and make the open web safer and freer for everyone.
